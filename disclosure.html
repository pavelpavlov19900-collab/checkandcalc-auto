Affiliate & Ads Disclosure

This page explains how Check&Calc Tools earns money and how advertising and affiliate links work on this website.

1. Why this disclosure exists

We believe in being transparent about how this site is funded. Check&Calc Tools is free to use, but running and improving the tools takes time and resources. To keep the site free, we use display advertising and may include affiliate links to products or services we recommend.

2. Display advertising (Google AdSense)

This website displays ads served by Google AdSense and possibly other ad networks. These ads may be based on the content of the page, your approximate location, your interests or generic ad targeting. We do not personally choose every single ad that appears.

When you view or click on ads, we may earn a small commission or fee. This does not change the cost of any product or service for you.

3. Affiliate links

Some links on this site may be affiliate links. This means that if you click on a link to a product or service and then make a purchase, we may receive a small commission from the seller or provider. The price you pay remains the same.

We only aim to recommend tools, products or services that we believe are useful or relevant to our audience, based on our knowledge and research. However, you should always do your own due diligence before making a purchase decision.

4. No professional advice

The information and tools provided on this site are for informational purposes only. They do not constitute legal, financial or security advice. Always consult a qualified professional for advice regarding your specific situation.

5. Independence and objectivity

Compensation from ads or affiliate links does not change our goal of providing simple, helpful tools. We do not sell “guaranteed rankings” or fake reviews. However, you should assume that we may receive compensation for some recommendations or links.

6. Questions

If you have questions about this disclosure, you can contact us via the email address listed in the Privacy Policy.
